Interests

Aside from AI and programming I have a fairly wide range of interests but mostly centered around being active. One of my main interests is Climbing, this includes bouldering (so short routes with no ropes) aswell as lead climbing (climbing with ropes and clipping the rope in as you go). With the University of Nottingham being an especially 'sporty' university the climbing club is well equipped for all sorts of climbing. So this varies from weekly meet-ups at the local gym to internation climbing trips (most recently El Chorro in Spain)

Furthermore I have a strong passion for Mountain Biking whether that being riding local trails or making trips to places such as Andorra & Morzine to use the ski-uplifts that are adapted take bikes during the summer season

My interest in Mountain Biking extends beyond just riding bikes and into the technical side of the sport, looking at the new prototype components being produced by elite racing teams and how they can provide marginal gains to that team when racing
